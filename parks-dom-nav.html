National Parks FAQ's

Yellowstone National Park

  • How many people visit Yellowstone National Park?
    3.29 million visitors in 2022
  • How old is Yellowstone National Park?
    Since March 1, 1872
  • How many people are employed at Yellowstone National Park?
    734 permanent staff and seasonally employed at Yellowstone.
  • What types of jobs are available at Yellowstone National Park?
    Law enforcement, Tour guides, Bussing tables, Checking guests into hotels, Serving food.
